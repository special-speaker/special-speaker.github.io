Included in Let's Chat are reflections written about 27 of the 66 Chat episodes Nita has hosted, and today can be found on Apple and Spotify podcasts under the title "The Chat with Nita Evans."

On her radio/podcast show, Nita chats with guests about their daily faith walk in Jesus Christ, and each features a woman of strong faith. Also included in Let's Chat are 27 devotions written from Nita's life experiences.

At the end of each reflection and devotion is a doodle created by Suzanne Lammert, the book's illustrator. She incorporates each Chat guest's life verse. You will be astonished how Suzanne captures the essence of each reflection and devotion in her doodles.

The goal of Let's Chat is to lift the name of Jesus high! John 12:32 It is Nita's hope and prayer that you, too, will lift the name of Jesus high as you read and are encouraged by this newest devotional.

Legacy of Faith: Walking with Jesus Come What May, is Nita’s 40 day devotional. It is written with a unique view of one who has experienced many hardships in life, all the while, holding onto the loving and all powerful hand of Jesus.

Examples of these: her home was destroyed by a flood in 2008, for three years she provided care through to the eventual death of her spouse of 36 years to early onset Alzheimer’s disease, she delivered her first child stillborn and one parent died in an auto accident and the other to cancer, and more…

Each devotion comes from the heart of one who has witnessed the everyday faithfulness of God from the perspective of a follower of Jesus Christ. It offers hope and encouragement while going through the tough times of life leaning on Jesus.
